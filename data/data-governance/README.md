# Data governance

> **Status: Not yet implemented.** No business service or solution under this capability has been realised in the current catalogue. The planned services below are sourced from [foundations/capability-map.md](../../foundations/capability-map.md) and represent the intended scope.

Ensures that data sharing adheres to defined quality, metadata, and governance standards. It provides services like data lineage, data profiling, and data quality rules.

## Planned business services

- **Data lineage** — records end-to-end provenance to enable impact analysis, compliance evidence, and reproducibility.
- **Data profiling** — analyses datasets for structure, distributions, and anomalies to inform governance decisions.
- **Data quality rules** — defines and evaluates quality checks with reporting and remediation workflows.
