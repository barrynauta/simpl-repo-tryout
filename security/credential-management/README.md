# Credential management

Cryptographic signing and verifiable credential (VC) lifecycle services for Simpl-Open, ensuring non-repudiation and authenticity of self-descriptions and usage contracts.

## Business services

- [Signing](signing/README.md) — digital signing of self-descriptions and contracts using provider private keys.
- [VC issuance and verification](vc-issuance-verification/README.md) — issuance and management of verifiable credentials for usage contracts.
- [Wallet](wallet/README.md) — secure storage and presentation of verifiable credentials.
