# CSIRT

> **Status: Not yet implemented.** No business service or solution under this capability has been realised in the current catalogue. The planned services below are sourced from [foundations/capability-map.md](../../foundations/capability-map.md) and represent the intended scope.

Provides coordinated incident detection, response, and resolution services. It ensures operational readiness against threats, manages vulnerability disclosures, and leads recovery activities in case of security incidents.

## Planned business services

- [Incident response](incident-response/README.md) — coordinates detection, containment, eradication, and recovery with post-incident review. _(Roadmap: TheHive.)_
- [Threat monitoring](threat-monitoring/README.md) — continuously monitors for indicators of compromise and emerging vulnerabilities. _(Roadmap: MISP.)_
