# Accessibility

## Overview

Accessibility in software refers to the design of products, devices, services, or environments that are usable by people with a wide range of abilities, including those with disabilities.

Ensuring that the system is usable by a broad range of users, enhanced documentation.

Ensure equal access for all users, including those with disabilities, promoting inclusivity and social responsibility.
