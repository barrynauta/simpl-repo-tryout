## 12B.9 - A Participant reports on their Simpl-Open agent usage, consumption of resources, and business actions

Description Simpl-Open shall allow a Participant to report on: The consumption of infrastructure resources. The consumption of data and application resources. Business actions. The usage of Simpl-Open agent components. The health of Simpl-Open agent components.
