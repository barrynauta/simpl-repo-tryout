## Overview

## 12B.1 - A Consumer and Provider log the consumption of infrastructure resources

Description Simpl-Open shall support a Provider and Consumer to collect metrics about the consumption of infrastructure resources, and store them in the central logs repository of their local agent: For Providers, this is referring to the infrastructure resources they are offering in the catalogue.
