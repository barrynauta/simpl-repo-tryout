## 9A.6 - Subscription to a data/application/infrastructure - Provider

Description A data/application/infrastructure Consumer can at any moment stop its subscription. If a data/application/infrastructure Provider changes the terms of the agreement for a dataset/application/infrastructure, the subscribed data/application/infrastructure Consumers are informed and have to again agree on the terms or the subscription stops.
