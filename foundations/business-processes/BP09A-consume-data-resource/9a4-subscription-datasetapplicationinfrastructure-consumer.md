## Overview

None.

## 9A.4 - Subscription to a dataset/application/infrastructure - Consumer

Description Simpl shall provide a UI and API: - to allow the consumer to subscribe to a dataset/application/infrastructure. This includes showing the parameters of the subscription like the frequency (continuous, with each update, with a certain frequency (hourly, daily, etc.) and duration. - to enable the consumer to establish a usage contract based on the subscription’s configuration - provide access the dataset/application/infrastructure to the end user as stipulated in the usage contract - to enable the Consumer to manage their subscriptions of dataset/application/infrastructure.
