# BP01 - Define Dataspace Governance

> **Status: Work In Progress** — detailed steps not yet fully published on the public site.

## Overview

This foundational process involves establishing the legal and technical framework for the data space. It defines the rules of participation, roles, and associated permissions that will govern all activities within the ecosystem.

## Core Objectives

-   Provide the necessary tools to establish a data space.
-   Define a clear structure of roles (Governance Authority, Provider, Consumer, End-User).
-   Establish the technical framework for role creation and permission management.

## Actors

-   **Governance Authority:** The central entity responsible for setting up the data space, defining roles, and managing permissions.

## Sequence of Steps (High-Level)

1.  **Definition of Governance Rules:** Establishing the legal and technical framework.
2.  **Creation of Roles:** Defining specific roles and their respective permissions.
3.  **Configuration of Governance Authority (BP02):** Setting up the technical instance of the authority.
4.  **Onboarding (BP03A/B):** Allowing participants and end-users to join under the defined rules.
