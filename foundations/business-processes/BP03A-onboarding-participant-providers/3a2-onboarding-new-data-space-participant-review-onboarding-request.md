## 3A.2 - Onboarding of a new data space participant - review of the onboarding request

Description Simple shall provide support for the review of the onboarding requests as follows. Depending on the rules set by the Data Space Governance Authority,  + Either the request shall be handled automatically  + Or the Data Space Governance Authority is notified of the new request for onboarding. In this case, Simpl shall provide a UI so that the Data Space Governance Authority can review the documents and approve or reject the participation.
