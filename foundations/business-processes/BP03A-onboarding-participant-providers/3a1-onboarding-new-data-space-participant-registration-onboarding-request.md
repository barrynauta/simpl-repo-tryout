## Overview

None.

## 3A.1 - Onboarding of a new data space participant - registration of onboarding request

<h4>Description</h4><p>Simpl should allow the Dataspace Governance Authority to define rules for the onboarding process such as:</p><ul><li>Simpl shall provide support when an organisation wants to request to participate to a data space with a certain role (Consumer, Data/Application/Infrastructure Provider) or a combination of these.</li><li>Simpl shall, a.o. request the data and documents required to verify the request.</li><li>When the requesting parties provide these, Simpl shall store them, and guide them through the submission of the onboarding request.</li></ul><p> </p><figure
