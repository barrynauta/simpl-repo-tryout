## 8.8 - Catalogue of services

Description Simpl shall provide a catalogue of infrastructure-related services, cloud, HPC, edge, defined under industry or de facto standards. The services shall range from IaaS-based services (e.g., VM, compute, storage) to more advanced SaaS services.
