# Policy Template Datastore

Stores contract and access policy templates that serve as blueprints when providers define usage policies for their resources. Templates held in this datastore are made accessible to consumers during resource negotiation and access stages.

Capability-map placement: `governance / policy-management / policy-administration-point / policy-template-datastore`. This solution implements the **Policy administration point** business service.

Provenance: planned — Catalogue & Connector team (no current source repository). Implemented with XFSC Organisation Credential Manager. Licence: Apache 2.0 (XFSC OCM upstream).
