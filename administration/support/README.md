# Support

> **Status: Not yet implemented.** No business service or solution under this capability has been realised in the current catalogue. The planned services below are sourced from [foundations/capability-map.md](../../foundations/capability-map.md) and represent the intended scope.

Provides operational assistance to participants and end-users through service desk services, ticketing systems, and status pages. It enables troubleshooting, issue tracking, and knowledge sharing to ensure smooth installation, configuration, and ongoing use of Simpl-Open components.

## Planned business services

- [Service desk](service-desk/README.md) — provides first-line assistance, triage, and knowledge-base guidance for participants and operators. _(Not yet implemented; no designated solution.)_
- [Support page](support-page/README.md) — publishes status, FAQs, runbooks, and contact channels to streamline self-service support. _(Not yet implemented; no designated solution.)_
- [Ticketing system](ticketing-system/README.md) — orchestrates issue lifecycle with SLAs, prioritisation, and handoffs across resolver groups. _(Roadmap: UVdesk.)_
